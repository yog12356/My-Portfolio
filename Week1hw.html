Cuneiform is a system of writing first devloped by the ancient Sumerians of Mesopotamia. This is considered the most significant contribution to modern-day. But this type of writing advanced as the years went by.

The earliest cuneiform tablets were known as proto-cuneiform. Later on the representation for cuneiform were simplified. They used a stylus to write down this type of writing. A sylus was a stick with a triangle at the end of it.
